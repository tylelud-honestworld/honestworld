Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

HonestWorld (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. We built HonestWorld on the principle that your personal data belongs to you and to no one else.

1. Information We Collect

Information you voluntarily provide: If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this solely to respond to your enquiry.

App usage: Barcode scans are processed to retrieve product information. Scan history is stored locally on your device. We do not associate your scans with any personal identifier on our servers.

Health profile: Any allergen preferences or dietary restrictions you configure are stored exclusively on your device. This information is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, payment details, device identifiers for tracking purposes, browsing history outside our app, or location data. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not fingerprint your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal information we receive is used only to operate and improve the service: to respond to support requests, to add submitted products to the database, and to maintain the technical integrity of the app. We use your data for no other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party under any circumstances. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, analytics companies, or any other commercial entity. We may share data only where required by law, and only to the minimum extent required.

5. Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect the limited data we hold. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take your data security seriously and maintain reasonable safeguards.

6. Data Retention

Support emails are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry. Product submission data is retained as part of the product database. We do not retain personal data beyond what is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@honestworldapp.com and we will respond promptly.

8. Children’s Privacy

HonestWorld is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or on this website. Continued use of HonestWorld after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please email us at support@honestworldapp.com. We take all privacy enquiries seriously and will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how HonestWorld uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. We believe in being straightforward about this: we use the bare minimum, and we never use cookies for advertising or tracking you.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language or settings, to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. Cookies We Use

Theme preference cookie (essential): When you switch between light and dark mode, we store your preference in your browser’s local storage so the site remembers your choice on your next visit. This stores only a single value (“light” or “dark”) and contains no personal information whatsoever.

That is the only cookie-like storage mechanism we use. There are no others.

3. Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use any of the following:

  • Analytics cookies — we do not track page views, session duration, or user behaviour
  • Advertising cookies — we show no ads and have no advertising partners
  • Social media cookies — we have no embedded social media tracking
  • Third-party cookies — no third party sets cookies through our website
  • Marketing or retargeting cookies — we do not follow you around the internet

4. Why We Keep It This Way

HonestWorld is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. Using tracking cookies or analytics tools would contradict the core values of the product. We have no interest in your browsing behaviour beyond what is strictly necessary to display the site correctly.

5. Managing Cookies

You can clear or block cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a cookie is being set. Blocking or clearing our theme preference storage will not affect your ability to use the site — you will simply need to reset your theme preference on your next visit.

For instructions on managing cookies in your specific browser, visit your browser’s help documentation.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we use cookies, we will update this policy and note the date of the revision. Given our minimal approach, significant changes are unlikely.

7. Contact

Questions about our cookie practices? Email us at support@honestworldapp.com and we will be happy to answer.
